Paint

A collection of water-based paintings that speaks to my intuitive art process - The combinations of repetitive forms, organic shapes, and vibrant colors arise from spontaneous play and my internal responses to thinking and feeling in the abstract

Bullseye

Gouache | Spring 2018 | 3' x 3'

Inspired by the work of Eric Sall, Bullseye arises as a response to its sister piece, Unbridled. It examines a balance between chaos and order, inviting viewers to get lost in its waves while also redemanding their attention through its striking bullseyes. This flow created between geometric black-and-white shapes and colorful, organic forms brings a dream-like, whimsical quality to the work.
