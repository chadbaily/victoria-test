About the Artist

Victoria Alvarez is a recent graduate of the University of Virginia with a B.S.Ed in Youth and Social Innovation and a Studio Art minor. Originally from Peachtree City, GA, Victoria cherishes her hometown as the place where she began nurturing her interest in art. She has continued to develop her passion and skill set through formal training with the Universityâ€™s Studio Art program. Though she has gained experience creating in various mediums, Victoria finds her strongest avenues for expression through sculpture, painting, and design. Victoria is a Miller Arts Scholar with a Visual Arts concentration, a role that has allowed her to explore creative collaboration with a network of peer artists. Her work has been featured in the student-run UVA Arts Magazine, V Magazine, and shown at the McGuffey Art Center in Charlottesville, VA.
