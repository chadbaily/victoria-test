Sculpt

Topo

Scrap wood, twine, mirrors | Fall 2019 | Life-size replication

Topo is a wearable wood sculpture inspired by outdoor landscapes and features of topographic maps. Its different elements - stacked arm slices, cascading mountains, mirrors, and directional arrows - come together to describe my all-consuming relationship with the outdoors. The elements serve as tokens of my experiences and adventures outside, preserving my memories of these outdoor landscapes in a physical framework. Topo appears to grow out of my body, almost in a parasitic fashion, which symbolizes how my affinity for the outdoors has taken over my physical and spiritual self.
