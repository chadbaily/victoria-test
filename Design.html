Reclaimed: Virtual Art Show

Adobe Illustrator | Spring 2020

RECLAIMED is a virtual art show that serves to commemorates my time as an artist at the University of Virginia. Works included in the document were originally intended for display in Charlottesville, VA at the IX Art Park. Initially scheduled for May 2020, this show was unfortunately CANCELLED due to COVID-19 circumstances.

This virtual show aims to honor this lost opportunity and bring my in-progress sketches, plans, and projects to life. It marks a celebration of my transition from creating as a student artist to engaging with the world as a lifelong creative.

WALK Capstone Innovation

Adobe Illustrator | Fall 2019

Created as a Capstone project for the Youth and Social Innovation major at the University of Virginia, this brief tracks my process in designing an innovation. The proposed innovation aimed to revamp the WALK Alternative Education Program in Charlottesville High School. I designed the brief to facilitate stakeholder buy-in and increase understanding through creatively-defined problems and solutions.

The project was presented to WALK program stakeholders, including Charlottesville schools Superintendent Rosa Atkins, in December 2019.
